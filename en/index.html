Frequently asked questions

Got a question? You may find the answer here

How do you provide a free offer?

We move on to the loan of a provider. The provider takes care of hosting your site. We act as a bridge between you and the hosting company. We have no costs to charge you. Our supplier takes care of all the technical aspects of the infrastructure, and we take care of the commercial aspects and after-sales service. To be honest, we only pay for the domain and site hosting. We don't have to pay any staff - we're passionate volunteers. Our provider earns money from domain sales and hosting improvements. We want to be transparent with you: we earn a commission, which is entirely reinvested in the project.

How did the project come about?

This project was born during the initial containment of the coronavirus pandemic. At the time, it was called FreeWebs. We were just two young teenagers whose ambition was to offer small retailers a solution for promoting their business and selling online. Unfortunately, we didn't see eye to eye and went our separate ways. Today, our new team has learned from past mistakes, and has rebuilt the project with greater ambition.

Why do I have to pay for paid offers on an external site?

The fact that we offer paying services means that we have to set up a secure payment platform. Our partner iFastNet, which offers these services, has all the necessary means to secure your order. Your paid service is managed entirely from their site, with all the necessary tools. Your offer is not linked to your Bees Tech account, but to your iFastNet account.
